# Project Purpose

ChargedParking is a commercial platform designed to help hotels, restaurants, business centers and commercial property owners increase property value by deploying electric vehicle charging infrastructure.

The platform combines business consulting, charging station deployment, customer management and digital services into a single ecosystem.

---

# Business Goal

Increase the attractiveness and competitiveness of commercial properties by providing EV Ready solutions.

The project focuses on creating measurable business value rather than simply selling charging equipment.
