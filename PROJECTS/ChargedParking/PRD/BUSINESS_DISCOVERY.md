# Project Vision

ChargedParking is a business platform that helps commercial property owners increase the value of their properties by integrating electric vehicle charging infrastructure into their business model.

The platform is not focused on selling charging stations.

Its primary objective is to increase customer traffic, improve competitiveness and create new revenue opportunities for property owners.

ChargedParking combines consulting, infrastructure planning, equipment selection, project management and long term customer support into a single commercial solution.

---

# Mission

Help businesses become EV Ready while creating measurable commercial value.

The company sells business outcomes rather than hardware.

---

# Long Term Vision

Become the leading EV Ready consulting and implementation company for commercial real estate in Russia.

The business should be recognized as a strategic partner that helps property owners prepare for the future of electric mobility.
---

# Business Problem

Commercial property owners increasingly face new customer expectations driven by the growth of electric vehicles.

Hotels, restaurants, shopping centers and business centers risk losing customers if they cannot provide convenient charging facilities.

Most property owners do not know:

How many charging stations are needed.

What equipment to choose.

How to estimate return on investment.

How to organize installation.

How to operate charging infrastructure.

How to promote EV Ready services.

As a result, many investment decisions are delayed or never implemented.

---

# Market Opportunity

Electric vehicle adoption continues to grow.

Governments support charging infrastructure.

Businesses compete for customers by offering additional services.

Commercial properties that become EV Ready gain a competitive advantage by attracting new visitors and increasing customer loyalty.

Charging infrastructure is becoming part of modern commercial real estate rather than an optional feature.

---

# Existing Alternatives

Property owners currently have several options.

Purchase charging stations directly from equipment suppliers.

Hire independent installation companies.

Work with electrical contractors.

Develop solutions internally.

These approaches typically solve only the technical installation problem.

They rarely address business strategy, customer experience, operational management and long term commercial value.

ChargedParking positions itself as a business transformation partner rather than an equipment supplier.
---

# Value Proposition

ChargedParking helps commercial property owners increase the value of their business by transforming parking spaces into modern EV Ready infrastructure.

The company does not simply install charging stations.

It delivers a complete business solution.

This includes business consulting, infrastructure planning, equipment selection, installation management, launch support and long term operational guidance.

The primary value is not the charging equipment itself.

The primary value is increased competitiveness, improved customer experience, stronger market positioning and preparation for the future growth of electric mobility.

Customers invest in business development rather than electrical equipment.
