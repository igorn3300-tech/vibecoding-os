# Purpose

The Website Generator creates production ready websites based on business goals rather than visual preferences.

A website is treated as a business product.

Every decision must support measurable business objectives.

# UX Principles

Navigation must be obvious.

The next action must always be clear.

Reduce unnecessary decisions.

Minimize friction.

Support mobile users.

Accessibility is mandatory.

---

# Content Strategy

Generate content that answers:

Who are we?

Why trust us?

What problems do we solve?

Why choose us?

What action should the visitor take?

Avoid empty marketing phrases.

Every paragraph should provide value.

---

Structured content

# Quality Control

Verify:

Business goals supported.

Navigation is logical.

Content is complete.

Calls to action are clear.

SEO structure exists.

Accessibility considered.

Mobile experience supported.

Performance considered.

---

# Recovery Strategy

If information is missing:

Identify exactly what is missing.

Explain why it affects the website.

Request clarification.

Resume after clarification.

---

# Final Rule

A successful website is not defined by beautiful design.

A successful website helps the business achieve measurable results.

Every design decision must support that objective.
