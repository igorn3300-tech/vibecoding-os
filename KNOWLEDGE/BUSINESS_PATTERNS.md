VibeCoding OS Business Knowledge

Version: 1.0 MVP

Status: APPROVED

---

# Purpose

This document contains reusable business patterns used by VibeCoding OS during product discovery, planning and architecture.

The objective is to improve business decisions before software design begins.

Technology should always follow business strategy.

---

# Core Principle

Every successful product solves a real business problem.

Features are not products.

Technology is not value.

Business value is the primary objective.

---

# Business Discovery Pattern

Always identify:

Business model

Target customers

Core problem

Current solution

Desired outcome

Revenue source

Success metrics

Growth opportunities

Never begin architecture before understanding these elements.

---

# Problem First Pattern

Every project starts with a problem.

The problem must be:

Real

Measurable

Frequent

Expensive

Worth solving

If no meaningful problem exists, the product should be reconsidered.

---

# Customer Value Pattern

For every feature ask:

What value does it create?

Who benefits?

Why would someone pay for it?

How is success measured?

If value cannot be explained, the feature should be challenged.

---

# MVP Pattern

The first version should solve one important problem extremely well.

Avoid solving many problems poorly.

Prioritize simplicity.

---

# Feature Prioritization Pattern

Every feature belongs to one category.

Critical

High

Medium

Low

Future

Future features should never delay MVP delivery.

---

# Competitive Analysis Pattern

Study competitors to understand:

Strengths

Weaknesses

Missing opportunities

Customer expectations

Never copy competitors.

Identify gaps instead.

---

# Business Risk Pattern

Evaluate:

Market risk

Financial risk

Operational risk

Legal risk

Technical risk

Reputation risk

Every significant risk should include a mitigation strategy.

---

# Decision Pattern

When several solutions exist evaluate:

Business Value

Implementation Cost

Time

Risk

Scalability

Maintainability

The best balanced solution should be selected.

---

# Product Validation Pattern

Before development confirm:

The customer exists.

The problem exists.

The customer is willing to change.

The solution creates value.

The business model is realistic.

---

# Long Term Thinking

Every recommendation should consider:

Scalability

Operational simplicity

Maintainability

Future growth

Customer satisfaction

Recurring value

---

# Final Principle

Business success is measured by customer value rather than technical complexity.

Every technical decision should support a business objective.

---

End of Business Patterns
