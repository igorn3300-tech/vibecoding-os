# Purpose

This document defines the functional and non functional requirements for the ChargedParking Business Operating Platform.

The objective is to provide developers, designers and AI systems with a complete specification for building a production ready solution.

---

# Product Vision

ChargedParking is a Business Operating Platform that helps commercial property owners plan, implement and manage EV Ready infrastructure projects.

The platform supports the complete business lifecycle from lead generation through project delivery and long term customer support.

---

# Product Objectives

Provide a professional digital platform for the ChargedParking business.

Increase operational efficiency.

Standardize project execution.

Improve customer experience.

Support company growth.

Create a scalable platform for future expansion.
